River Lýsa FAQ

What skill level do I need to have?

River Lýsa is perfect for everyone, from beginners to seasoned anglers. It’s a family-friendly fly fishing haven and a popular spot for landing your very first catch. For experienced anglers, the river offers a technical challenge, from longer lake casting to deep river euro-nymphing. There’s something for everyone!

What do I need to bring?

We’ve got you covered! Our guided tours include food, water, rods, waders, flies, leaders, and, of course, an expert guide. We recommend packing warm clothing—even in the summer. Iceland’s weather is as adventurous as its landscapes.

Can I customize our tour?

Absolutely! We work with top travel agencies in Iceland and can expand your adventure to include other fly fishing hotspots or exciting activities like highland trekking, snowmobiling, helicopter rides, or whatever else you can dream up. Let us know your vision, and we’ll make it happen!

Are flights included?

Flights are not included in our prices. We recommend booking your flights independently to ensure the best fit. That said, we’re here to help! If you need assistance with flight arrangements, we’re happy to provide guidance and support.
